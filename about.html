Our story

Built by educators who wanted better tools for their own students.

Prep Portal is developed by a team of tech educators in Nigeria — a subsidiary of Superbrains Nigeria — building original, exam-style practice for WAEC, NECO, JAMB and Cambridge learners.

Why we built Prep Portal

Every one of us has stood in front of a classroom or tutored a student who was ready to give up — not because they weren't capable, but because good practice material was hard to find, scattered across old photocopies, or locked behind tools that weren't built for how Nigerian students actually study. We built Prep Portal to fix that: one place with thousands of original, exam-style questions, AI-marked theory and writing practice, and interactive tools that make revision feel less like a chore.

Part of Superbrains Nigeria

Prep Portal is a subsidiary of Superbrains Nigeria, and shares its parent company's mission: using technology to raise the quality of education available to Nigerian students, wherever they are.

What we care about

Original content

Every question is written in-house, in the style of real exams — never copied from past papers.

Built for students first

Every feature is judged by one question: does this actually help a student pass their exam?

Quality over shortcuts

AI-marked feedback, worked solutions and interactive labs — built to teach, not just quiz.

Always improving

We ship new subjects, activities and tools continuously based on what students and teachers ask for.
