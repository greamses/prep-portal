About us

Everything a Nigerian student needs to prepare for an exam, in one place.

Prep Portal is developed by a team of tech educators in Nigeria — a subsidiary of Superbrains Nigeria. We combine an original CBT question bank across 20+ subjects, AI-marked theory and writing feedback, interactive labs and games, and one-on-one tutoring into a single platform for WAEC, NECO, JAMB, Cambridge and SAT preparation.

Our story

Every one of us has stood in front of a classroom or tutored a student who was ready to give up — not because they weren't capable, but because good practice material was hard to find, scattered across old photocopies, or locked behind tools that weren't built for how Nigerian students actually study. We built Prep Portal to fix that: one place with thousands of original, exam-style questions, AI-marked practice, and interactive tools that make revision feel less like a chore — backed, when a student needs it, by a real human tutor.

What's on Prep Portal

The platform is built around five pillars: practice exams, AI-marked feedback, interactive learning, original articles, and human tutoring. Here's what's inside each.

Practice exams

Our CBT engine covers 20+ subjects — Mathematics, English Language, Biology, Physics, Chemistry, Economics, Government, Commerce, Geography, Christian and Islamic Religious Studies, Literature in English, Financial Accounting, History, Civic Education, Agricultural Science, Quantitative and Verbal Reasoning, General Studies, Further Mathematics, ICT and Business Studies — styled after the exams students actually sit:

Common Entrance UTME WASSCE Post-UTME SAT IGCSE A-Level

Papers are auto-capped at 60 questions and marked instantly, with a free mixed-difficulty Practice mode for students who just want to drill a subject without picking an exam style. Every question is written in-house in the style of real past papers — never copied verbatim — so the content stays original while still matching what students will see on exam day.

AI-marked feedback

Beyond multiple choice, Prep Portal grades the harder stuff:

  • Theory Practice — long-form theory and essay questions marked by AI, with a full model answer and explanation, not just a score.
  • Writing Evaluator — essays and compositions graded for grammar, structure and rubric criteria, with inline red-pen-style annotations.
  • PrepBot — an AI study assistant students can chat with for explanations, hints and worked examples while they revise.
  • AI Flashcards — auto-generated, spaced-repetition flashcard decks for quick recall practice.

Interactive learning

For subjects that click better hands-on, we've built a set of visual, interactive tools: an Algebra Lab for drag-and-drop equation solving, activities for equivalent fractions, polygon angles, surface area and transversals, a Cartesian Art studio for coordinate-plotting, and a Graph Predictor for functions. Our Virtual Lab brings 3D chemistry, physics and biology simulations you can explore in first- or third-person, and a library of 2D and 3D games — like a bearings-teaching drone delivery game and a third-person 3D maze — turn practice into play.

Original articles

Our blogs publish original science articles — Plant Facts and Animal Facts collections written specifically for each class level from Primary 1 through SS3 — plus quick math tricks, all free to read.
