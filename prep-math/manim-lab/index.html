Animated Math — Hybrid Engine

Proof-of-concept: the symbolic solution (MathJax) and the graph (canvas) animate together from one GSAP timeline — the "manim-style" hybrid. This scene is a parametric template for linear equations ax + b = c; pick a preset or type your own.

Tip: the dot rides up the line y = ax + b until it meets y = c — the x at that point is the solution.
