Privacy Policy

Last updated: 6 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Prep Portal ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and safeguards your information when you use prepportal.com.ng and our related services (the "Service"). By using the Service you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information we collect

  • Account details — your name, email address, and authentication data when you create an account or sign in.
  • Usage data — pages visited, questions attempted, progress, and similar activity used to improve your experience.
  • Payment data — handled by our payment processors; we do not store full card details on our servers.
  • Technical data — device, browser, and approximate location derived from your IP address.

2. How we use your information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the Service.
  • To personalise content and track your study progress.
  • To process subscriptions and tutoring bookings.
  • To communicate updates, support responses, and important notices.
  • To detect, prevent, and address abuse or security issues.

3. Sharing your information

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with service providers who help us operate the Service (such as hosting, analytics, and payment processing), and where required by law.

4. Data retention

We keep your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

5. Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data, and you may close your account at any time. To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below.

6. Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to keep you signed in, remember preferences, and understand how the Service is used. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

7. Children's privacy

Prep Portal is intended for students. Where required, a parent or guardian should review this policy and supervise the use of the Service by younger users.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be posted on this page with a new "last updated" date.
